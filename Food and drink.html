Belgio is Bergen’s first – and only – Belgian bar. The breadth
and diversity of Belgian beer culture has led to it being placed
on UNESCO’s cultural heritage list. We have a wide selection
and serve everything from tart Lambic beers to the more
mellow blond ales.
